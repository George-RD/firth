Barbara Liskov and Jeannette Wing, "A Behavioral Notion of Subtyping", ACM
Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems 16(6), 1994. The
paper formalises substitutability through contravariant arguments, covariant
results, preconditions that admit every supertype call, and postconditions that
preserve every supertype guarantee. Its stronger preservation of arbitrary
history properties requires equal corresponding preconditions, so ordinary
precondition weakening alone must not be presented as sufficient for that
claim.
