Product Requirements Document v0.1 for the Firth programming language. Defines the project vision (a concatenative language in the Forth tradition with Lean 4 elaboration and machine-checked guarantees, designed for machine authorship as a first-class constraint), nine goals G1–G9, non-goals, four scope layers (language, toolchain, runtime, ecosystem), seventeen requirements R1–R17, seven success criteria S1–S7, risks and open questions, licensing posture (Apache-2.0 toolchain and specifications; MIT/Apache-2.0 dual for runtime and standard library), and the artefact chain from PRD through kernel specification and Lean mechanisation to component specifications.
