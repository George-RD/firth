Barbara Liskov and Jeannette Wing, "A Behavioral Notion of Subtyping", ACM
Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems 16(6), 1994. The
paper formalises substitutability through contravariant arguments, covariant
results, preconditions that admit every supertype call, postconditions that
preserve every supertype guarantee, and preservation of invariants and history
properties.
